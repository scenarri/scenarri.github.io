Education

 I am currently pursuing the Ph.D degree in Information & Communication Engineering at the Comprehensive Situation Awareness Group, College of Electronic Science and Technology, NUDT, fortunately supervised by Prof. Xiang Li and Prof. Li Liu. I received my M. Eng. (Supervisor: Prof. Shaowei Yong, Associate Prof. Bo Peng, and Prof. Li Liu) and B. Eng. degrees at NUDT, in 2022 and 2020, respectively.

Research Interest

 My research interests include computer vision, adversarial machine learning, remote sensing image recognition (especially SAR imagery), and multimodal foundation models for remote sensing. Currently, I focus on:
  • Adversarial attack/robustness of vision models
  • Understanding and trustworthiness of foundation models for remote sensing
